Viking Bread

Description

A hearty and delicious bread that will go with many recipes.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups of whole wheat flour
  • 2 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup of old-fashioned oats
  • 1 tsp of baking soda
  • 1 tsp of salt
  • 2 cups of water

Steps

  1. Combine whole wheat flour, all-purpose flour, oats, baking soda and salt
  2. Add water and stir with wooden spoon
  3. Knead the dough for a few minutes
  4. Divide into 6 even balls
  5. Place balls on baking sheet
  6. Place baking sheet in over and turn on to 375 F
  7. Bake for 20-30 minutes
  8. Take baking sheet and let it cool for a few minutes
