Beef Stew with Vegetables

Description

A classic stew the upcoming chilly months. It doubles beautifully.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb of stew meat
  • 1/4 cup of all-purpose flour
  • 5 Tbsp of Oil
  • 2 Tbsp of Red Wine Vinegar
  • 1 cup of Red Wine
  • 3.5 cups of Beef broth
  • 1 medium chopped onion
  • 5 medium carrots
  • 2 baking potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 2 tsp of Salt
  • 1/4 tsp of Black Pepper
  • 1 tsp of Paprika
  • 2 Bay leaves
  • Lemon Slices

Steps

  1. Combine flour and pepper
  2. Add beef and toss to coat well
  3. Heat 3 tsp of oil
  4. Add beef, don't overcook
  5. Brown beef on all sides, about 5 minutes per batch
  6. Once beef is cooked, add vinegar and wine to pot, cook over medium
  7. Add beef, broth and bay leaves
  8. Bring to a boil then reduce to simmer
  9. Cover and let cook for about 30 minutes
  10. Add onions and carrots, cook for 10 minutes
  11. Add potatoes and simmer until vegetables are tender, 30 minutes
  12. Season with salt and enjoy!
