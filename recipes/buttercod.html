Boiled Cod with Butter and Mustard

Description

A simple and quick recipe for Cod Fish that is seasoned and cooked in butter and mustard with herbs and fresh lemon.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lbs of Cod fish
  • 6 Tbsp of Butter
  • 2 Tbsp of Mustard
  • 1/4 tsp of Garlic Powder
  • 1/2 tsp of Salt
  • 1/4 tsp of Black Pepper
  • 1 tsp of Paprika
  • 1 tsp of Fresh Herbs
  • Lemon Slices

Steps

  1. Combine dry seasoning ingredients
  2. Clean Cod fish with cold water
  3. Cut fish into pieces
  4. Season all sides of the Cod with the cobined seasoning
  5. Combine mustard and Cod
  6. Heat 2 Tbsp of butter in larger skillet
  7. Once heated, cook Cod for 2 minutes
  8. Turn heat to medium and cook the other side for 3-4 minutes
  9. Add additional butter to liking
  10. Drizzle Cod with lemon juice
  11. Sprinkle herbs!
